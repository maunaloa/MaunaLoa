About Mauna Loa

San Rafael's wonderful family-owned restaurant featuring Hawaii's multi-ethnic convergence of cuisines. From barbeque beef short ribs to slow roasted Kalua pig, our Hawaiian menu offers a world of flavors for your choosing. Kids menu available. Open Monday - Saturday 11AM-9PM. Closed Sunday.

Reviews

Best wonton soup in Marin, delicious Hawaiian specialties, friendly service, convenient location.

"Hey Bruddah, been looking for da kine? I just found it. I can't believe with all the back and forth that Marinites have with the Islands that there's never been a Hawaiian joint in Marin. Well, here it is, just opened, and I think it's a winner. No poi, no luau just good plate lunches with the right sides: scoops of rice and macaroni salad. I opted for the chicken combo, which was equal parts teriyaki, bbq and katsu. Really moist and juicy. The staff was all really friendly and I wish these guys luck. The location is in a kind of dead stretch of 3rd St., but I hope this breathes new life into the 'hood." Mahalo." Jogn De, opening
